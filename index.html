Privacy Policy for 随时译

Last updated: September 13, 2025

This privacy policy governs your use of the 随时译 Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use

The 随时译 extension does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information about you, such as your name, email address, or browsing history.

The extension's sole purpose is to provide translation services for the web page you are currently viewing. The content of the page is sent to a third-party translation service to be translated, but this data is not associated with you or your account.

Permissions

The extension requires the "scripting" permission to access and modify the content of the web pages you visit, in order to provide the translation service.
